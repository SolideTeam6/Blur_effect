Vintage Photos by Canva

Vintage photography is nostalgic and elegant. It somehow manages to capture the look and feel of a bygone era, which makes it perfect for use in unique and creative design projects. But vintage photography is a skill that not many photographers can master. There’s so much skill and technique going into the compositions and the subjects that only professional photographers manager to get it right over and over.

Fortunately, Canva has a stock library filled with vintage photos — browse through the collection and choose one that will give your designs that nostalgic effect you need. From cobblestone streets to vintage wear to sepia-toned portraiture and everything in between, Canva’s vintage photography holds everything you need for your vintage-inspired design needs.

Every image in Canva is carefully reviewed and curated so you get access to only the best photos. Yet, it’s all cost-effective because you need only pay $1 for every one-time use of an individual image. You can download the image as a high-resolution JPEG or PNG file or open it in Canva and use it with any of the thousands of free design templates.

With the help of some easy to use design tools, you’ll have a perfect design ready in minutes. Each image comes ready made but you can always add your own enhancement or flair to it by using the photo editing tools. Play around with filters or add more effects like vignette, blur and x-process or adjust image settings like contrast, brightness and saturation to perfection.
