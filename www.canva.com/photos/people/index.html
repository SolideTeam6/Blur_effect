People Photos by Canva

Need access to a stock photo library of people, to use in marketing materials or other Canva designs?

If you’ve used Canva in the past, you’ll know we make sure that everyone who creates with us has all the visual tools they need to complete their design projects. This includes stock photography. Browse through Canva’s stock photo library of people and choose one that’s perfect for your design.

There are many reasons why you would want or need to add an image of a person or persons in your design, one of which is to make your message more relatable. With Canva’s wide and diverse collection of people photography, you’re sure to find the image that fits your needs. Browse through portraits of individuals, group shots, couples, friends, action shots or close-ups to find the right image.

Simply open your chosen image in Canva then start customizing through its easy to use design tools. Only high quality images are hosted in Canva so your designs will look brilliant whether printed or on the web.
