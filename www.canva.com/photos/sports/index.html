Sports Photos by Canva

Sport photography is a skill not too many photographers can master. It requires a keen sense of occasion and expert timing to ensure a successful shoot. Luckily, Canva has a network of professional stock sport photographers that get it right every time.

Browse through Canva’s collection of sport photos and choose one that will give your designs the lifelike effect you need. We have photos covering most major international sports (and heaps of smaller, national sports), so you’re bound to find the photos you need.

Canva can help you make your (design) goals with less time and costs because it gives you access to everything you need to create beautifully designed, professional layouts without having to hire a photographer or graphic artist. All you need is to choose the right sports image from its amazing stock photo library and the best design template for your needs (there are thousands of them!).

Once you’ve chosen an image, you can choose to download it in high-resolution JPEG or PNG or open it in Canva to create photo collages, posters, presentations, flyers, brochures and so much more. With its intuitive design tools, Canva can help you create a custom design in mere minutes. Use the photo editing tools to add a filter or effects to your photo then download it for print or web use.
