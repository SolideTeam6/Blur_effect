Animal and Wildlife Photos by Canva

Animal and wildlife photos are warm and vivid, and, when done properly, the subjects appear as if they’re literally jumping off the page.

If you’ve used Canva in the past, you’ll know how committed we are to ensuring that everyone who designs with us creates work that intrigues and captures the eye. Our library of animal and wildlife photos are used so often because they enable our users to achieve those goals.

Browse through Canva’s collection of animal and wildlife photos and choose one that will give your designs warmth and life. From amphibians to mammals, reptiles to birds, you have your pick of the best photos for your design needs. Canva hosts a wide collection of curated professional stock photography so whether you need shots of animals in action — running, flying, swimming, crawling, or at rest, you won’t need to look further.

Only the best quality images are hosted in Canva so you can be sure that any design you create will look great in any resolution, whether for web or for print. Every image is good to go but if you want to enhance it further, you can always add filters or adjust settings like brightness and contrast or even crop and scale the photo. Canva’s easy to use design tools ensure that you can customize any template and design element with just a few clicks.
