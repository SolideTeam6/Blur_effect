Interior Photos by Canva

As highly visual creatures, humans have developed a natural eye for design. We are quick to dismiss things that look unpleasant to us, putting effort into making sure that the things we see and create are aesthetically appealing. It’s no wonder, then, that a tremendous amount of time, thought, and effort goes into interior design. As they say, “Show me your home, and I’ll tell you who you are.” Whether at home or in the office, interior design definitely matters.

Demonstrate your good taste and put your creativity - and interior design skills! - to good use. Take a look at our collection of home and office interior photos and choose the perfect image for your Canva creations.

From baroque-inspired designs to modern interiors, from theaters to planes, apartments and offices — Canva hosts a wide collection of interior design photography. Plus, Canva’s interior photography ranges from wide-angle shots to close-up of details. Each image is reviewed and curated by our team armed with a strict set of guidelines. Canva helps you save costs too! Never pay for more than $1 for each one-time use of a photo.

Use your new photo on any design template in Canva by dragging and dropping it into any grid or frame. Canva also gives you access to easy to use design tools like advanced filters for enhancing your images. Play around with settings like brightness and contrast or choose from any of the filter settings. All images in Canva can also be cropped, scaled, rotated or resized as needed.
