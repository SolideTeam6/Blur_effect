Building and Landmark Photos by Canva

Buildings and manmade landmarks are a testament to what we’ve achieved as a civilization. We tend to get so preoccupied with our day-to-day activities that we take for granted the fact that we’ve created towering behemoths that pierce the skyline.

Adding snapshots of these impressive structures to your creative projects creates an overall “urban” feel to your output. More often than not, such photos are used as background images, helping to set the right mood and tone and allowing you to evoke the right emotions from your audience. Canva hosts a wide collection of building and landmark photography — from panoramic shots to photographs of building details. Each image is strictly reviewed and curated so only the best photos make it to you.

These images are ready to be integrated to any Canva template. Simply drag and drop your purchased image into any design layout. Use the grids or frames tool to organize your photos and the advanced filters tools to enhance your images. You can also scale, crop and resize photos as needed.

Imbue your Canva designs with a sense of depth and perspective by choosing the perfect backdrop from our collection of building and landmark photos. With thousands of professionally-shot stock photos to choose from, you’re sure to find what you need for your design. Plus, you won’t need to pay more than a $1 for a single use of these photos.
