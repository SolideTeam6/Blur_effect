Abstract Photos by Canva

Abstract photos are creative and interesting. Designers are forever on the lookout for visual elements that capture an audience, and abstract photos offer a simple way to do this.

Sometimes, photographers plan to achieve an abstract result in their work, but a lot of the time it’s done by accident. Either way, abstract photography is incredibly captivating and uses light, textures, colors and other visual elements to create striking effects. Browse through Canva’s collection of abstract photos and choose one that will give your designs impact and intrigue.

Ranging from a wide variety of subject matter and themes, abstract photos add an interesting visual component to any design — whether you’re creating a presentation, poster, book cover, flyer or any other graphic. Canva hosts only high quality images so you can be sure that your designs will look amazing in any resolution, whether for print or web. Plus, each image is carefully reviewed and curated according to a strict set of guidelines. You’re sure to never go over budget though, because you won’t have to pay more than $1 for each single-time use of a Canva image.

Once you’ve chosen an image, you can use it in any of the thousands of free design templates in Canva. With Canva’s intuitive interface and design tools, you can customize any template in minutes. Drag and drop your image into any template and use the grid or frame tool to contain your photo. Perfect your photo composition by cropping or scaling your image.
