The Arts Photos by Canva

Photos that express elements of the arts are renowned for their unique take on the world and the visual surrounds of the artist taking them.

Expressing subtle elements of life, imagination and creativity, “the arts” covers activities of creative skills, like music, painting, and literature. When these activities are captured in photography, they take the viewer on their own creative journey.

Browse through Canva’s collection of arts photos and choose one that will give your designs an avant-garde appeal. Taking inspiration from various art forms like architecture, literature, and more, these images are the perfect visual accompaniment to any book cover, poster, or collage design. Use your chosen image with any of Canva’s thousands of free templates and layouts to create a professional design in mere minutes.

Canva’s goal is to empower everyone to design so we’ve made all our design tools easy to use. In a few clicks, you can customize and edit each template to suit your needs and style. Drag and drop any image from Canva’s photo library into a design layout. Use the grids or frames tool to organize and contain your images and to crop, scale or resize them for better photo composition. Enhance or add more flair to your images by using the advanced filters tool. Adjust settings like brightness, contrast and saturation or choose among the pre-made filters.
