Parks and Outdoor Photos by Canva

Parks represent the happy marriage of urban life and nature. The hangout of choice of families, couples, and youngsters the world over, parks are the perfect place to relax, bond or to just revel in the beauty of nature, while still enjoying amenities from modern society.

There’s just something so refreshing about sitting outside, basking in the sunlight while enjoying scenic views. It’s a nice break from the hustle and bustle of everyday life, and a reminder that we should all take it easy every once in awhile. Need a photo collage featuring fun outdoor activities like tree climbing, skating or just strolling through tree-lined walkways? Or maybe you need to create a poster encouraging your local community to go outside and enjoy nature? Find exactly what you need by browsing through Canva’s wide collection of carefully curated and reviewed stock photos.

Add your chosen images to any design template in Canva and use the intuitive interface to edit and customize them further. Use the grids and frames tools to organize, crop and scale your photos and play around with the advanced filters tool to adjust image settings like brightness, contrast and saturation. Or, apply any of the pre-set filters to instantly add flair to your photos.

Choose from our collection of photos of parks and outdoor views and infuse your Canva designs and presentations with a unique blend of fun and serenity. Plus you won’t have to pay more than $1 for each one-time use of premium elements and images.
