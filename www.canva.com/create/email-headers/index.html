Get Attention with an Email Header

Start Designing an E-mail Header

Canva's amazingly simple drag-and-drop design tool makes it easy to create an email header that will be noticed, read, and treasured.

Make a great first impression with a striking email header

Long gone are the days when a great email was text alone — today’s e-mails need to snap, crackle, and pop. Whether you’re composing a marketing email for work or writing a personal note with a little extra love and care, Canva can help you put together a stylish email header to adorn the top of your correspondence.

Open a new E-mail Header Design

Create a professional email header in under five minutes

  1. Open Canva and select the “Email Header” design type.
  2. Choose from our library of beautifully designed layouts.
  3. Upload your own photos or choose from over 1 million stock images.
  4. Fix your images, add stunning filters and edit text.
  5. Save and share.

Customize your email header and make a splash

  1. Change the images. Upload your own images or choose from our stock library of over 1 million photographs, graphics and illustrations.
  2. Change the fonts. Choose from over 130 fresh fonts.
  3. Change the background. Choose a background from our library or use an image.
  4. Change the colors. Change the color of your text boxes and text to add extra flair.

Keep people reading with powerful design

Are you a wordsmith but not an artist? Can you write a sonnet but lack an eye for great fonts? No problem! Canva comes with tons of email header layouts to choose from that are bold, beautiful, and modern so you can focus on your writing.

Pick a layout, then upload your own photos to give your email header a personal touch. If you can’t find that perfect picture of a sunset in your own photo albums, Canva comes with over 1 million  stock photos and images to search through to perfectly complement your email. With plenty of free images to choose from, and premium images available for only $1, there’s no reason not to start your email strong.

Crank Out Newsletters Quick with Canva

Suppose you want to get the next issue of your popular caving newsletter out fast. With Canva it’s simple to duplicate your design, edit the parts you need, and have the email header for your next cavern-exploring issue ready to descend upon your readers.

Canva stores all your design projects in the cloud, so you can access, edit, and share them anywhere there is an internet connection. With Canva’s free iPad app you can even update your email headers on the go, or at the bottom of a cave (though you’ll probably have to come to the surface before you mail it out).

Spread your message everywhere

Why stop at email? Make sure you get the most out of your eye-catching email header by sharing it across all of your social media outlets. Simply click “Share” at the top right of the screen to post your design to Facebook or Twitter, or generate a link you can post anywhere.
