Craft the Perfect Gift Certificate for Any Occasion

Start Designing a Gift Certificate

Timely gift certificates make gift-giving a stress-free affair. With Canva, you can now design this go-to gem yourself with just a few clicks.

Design a gift certificate nobody would want to wrap up

How many gift-giving occasions are there in a year? A lot. But they don’t all have to provoke a mad rush to the mall and a look-through all of its stores. Just select your recipient’s favorite shop, purchase their gift certificate, and say goodbye to mismatched gifts for life!

With Canva’s tools and wide selection of professional layouts, now even non-designers can craft one of these gift favorites! Add your own photos or choose from our well-equipped picture library with over a million high-quality images. Now anyone can breathe new meaning to the phrase: “it’s the thought that counts.”

Open a New Gift Certificate Design

It only takes a few clicks to make stunning gift certificates

  1. Open Canva and select the Gift Certificate design type
  2. Choose from our library of professional graphic design elements such as banners, icons, frames and more.
  3. Upload your own photos or choose from over 1 million stock images.
  4. Fix your images, add stunning filters and edit text.
  5. Save and share.

Design and customize your gift certificate with ease

  1. Change the images. Upload your own images or choose from our stock library of over 1 million photographs, graphics and illustrations.
  2. Change the fonts. Choose from over 130 fresh fonts.
  3. Change the background. Choose a background from our library or use an image.
  4. Change the colors. Change the color of your text boxes and text to add extra flair.

Easily customize your gift certificate to match your brand

If just the thought of designing from scratch makes you cringe, take that chill pill and let us help you out.

With our selection of design elements, photos, and layouts you will most likely find ones that match your brand or the look that you’re going for. Customize with your brand’s colors and add all the details that you need: amount, validity dates, a space for names, or even a short message for the recipient. Whatever the look and feel of your brand – you can easily achieve it in Canva.

Beautiful gift certificates anyone can design

Whether you’re creating a gift certificate for a loved one’s birthday or rushing to meet a delivery date for your store, with Canva’s ready-to-use designs and simple tools you’d finish all your requirements in no time!

You may choose from our range of beautiful ready-to-use layouts, over 1 million stock images, illustrations, and icons! No worries if design is not your forte, we have a user-friendly interface that will allow you to layout easily according to your market’s personality and the look you have in mind. Your gift certificate will be saved in your Canva account for future use or you may revisit your design any time for any variations.

Easily collaborate with your whole team

Allow your marketing team to collaborate seamlessly with our interactive design platform. Everyone gets a chance to lend their design expertise and integrate their own ideas for the whole team’s review and input.

Just tap on the share button and send out an editable link to your team. Not only will they have access to Canva’s complete set of tools, they would also be able to upload their own photos or move around the design elements with ease. In Canva, all members of your team–writers, photographers, or even accountant–can be a designer.

Share your gift certificate online or print it out in stunning high resolution

Do you want to upload your gift certificate on your website for online purchase or send it printed via snail mail? No need to furrow  your brow, Canva’s nifty download button lets you do just that!

After designing your heart out, simply select the download button on your screen and save your file as an image or high-quality PDF. Voila! You now have a gift certificate you can upload on your website or print however many copies to mail to your most loyal clients, ready for use.
