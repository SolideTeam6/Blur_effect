Multimedia Icons by Canva

These days, brands are focusing their efforts on content, which includes everything from blogs and videos to music and photos. Creating these usually takes time and effort, but it doesn’t have to be that difficult. With Canva’s collection of multimedia icons, you’ll be able to compose effective marketing materials in a breeze.

Each icon was meticulously designed by our team of designers, so you won’t need an artistic background to use them. All you have to do is access our online editor via desktop, iPad, or iPhone. Decide on your layout type and search for media resources on your Canva workspace, or just scroll through this page to find what you need.

Our multimedia icons combine great design with the simplicity of vector illustrations. They also come in a variety of looks, like your basic camera symbol and the more detailed film reel. With their versatility, they’re ideal for company websites, presentations, or brochures. They also do wonders for your content’s readability. After all, a single emblem can convey your message in an instant.

Canva gives you the tools to modify these images to suit your style. You can switch up the colors using our handy palette, which provides color suggestions based on your document. You’re also free to resize or rotate elements with an easy drag-and-drop gesture. Adjust the opacity, transform them into clickable items, or make as many copies as you want. Our intuitive interface lets you take control of all your company’s visuals, so you can grow your business without the fuss.
