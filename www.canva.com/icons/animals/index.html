Animal Icons by Canva

For wild parties, jungle-themed baby showers, or zoo-inspired banners, you’ll definitely need Canva’s icons of animals. These colorful additions will go great with anything! After all, our collection features a variety of critters, such as adorable penguins and friendly koalas. Why not take a peek to get started?

Simply browse our selection of icons and choose your favorites. You can adorn your photo collages with puppy vectors. Create infographics and blog visuals about how to take care of cats. Can’t find the animal you’re looking for? Just type your keywords in Canva’s handy search bar.

You don’t need to be a professional designer to produce such eye-catching layouts. Just sign in to your free account and access our intuitive drag-and-drop interface. There, you can customize your chosen design and even tweak our range of icons. Go ahead and take advantage of all our handy tools to fine-tune your creations. You can resize or rotate icons to your heart’s content, or change their color schemes to match your own. If you want to get more creative, why not experiment with opacity by using our sliders? Combine icons to create lovely patterned backgrounds. With our user-friendly editor, you get hundreds of ways to create engaging materials for your event.

Icons are also best paired with typography. Lucky for you, our library has plenty of cool and unique typefaces. Just have fun with it! You’re bound to discover exciting new designs with Canva. And when you’re done, you can simply download your creations or share them with friends online.
